What is Bootleg?
It’s a website. You buy things. We send them. That’s the whole magic. I don't know what else to tell you.

How long does shipping take?
Somewhere between “soon” and “eventually.”. We say 3–10 business days, but life is unpredictable. I once ordered a toaster and it showed up 6 months later. Not from us, but still.

Can I return something?
Yeah. You can. Within 30 days. Don’t use it, don’t break it, don’t send it back covered in regret. Print a label, toss it in a box, hope someone at the warehouse isn’t on their break.

Where’s my tracking info?
You should get an email. If not, check spam. If it’s not there, I don’t know—maybe it got lost in the void. That happens. A lot. Tough luck.

What payments do you take?
Whatever works. Credit card, debit, probably PayPal. No crypto.

My item is broken.
So is my will to live. But yeah, email us a photo. We’ll try to sort it out. It helps only if you don’t scream in all caps.

Who do I contact?

Is there a store I can visit?
No. Just this site. You’re already in the store. This is it. Hope you're enjoying the ambiance.

Why “Bootleg”?
Someone thought it was cool in a meeting 12 days ago. No one stopped them. Here we are.

Are the products legit?
Look. They work. They do the thing they’re supposed to do. Is that not enough anymore?
